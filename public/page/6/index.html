cuts prints selected parts of a file. -d, delimiter -f, field Example grab all the usernames in /etc/passwd cut -f1 -d: /etc/passwd where field f1 is the first occurance before delimiter :. Since the first occurance is the username, the command above will grab all the usernames. cut -f7 -d: /etc/passwd Field 7 is the last part in /etc/passwd, i.e, the shell associated with each account.
